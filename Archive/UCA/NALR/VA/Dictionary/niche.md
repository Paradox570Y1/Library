The word **"niche"** refers to a **specialized segment** of a market, role, or area that is **well-suited to a particular person, product, or interest**. It’s something **very specific** rather than general or broad.

---

### 🔍 **Meaning in simple terms:**

A **niche** is a **specific space or role** where something fits perfectly and can thrive, often because it’s **tailored to a specific need** or audience.

---

### ✅ **Perfect example to explain:**

> Imagine you're starting a **YouTube channel**.  
> You could make videos about **"fitness"** (a broad topic), or you could make videos about **"home workouts for busy moms over 40"** — that’s a **niche**.

Why?  
Because it targets a **very specific audience** with **specific needs**, making it easier to connect deeply with viewers who relate to it.

---

### 💡 Real-world niche examples:

- A clothing brand that only makes **eco-friendly yoga wear for plus-size women**
- A bakery that only sells **sugar-free, vegan cupcakes**
- A tech blog focused only on **Android customization tips**
- A travel Instagram page that only posts about **hidden trekking routes in Himachal Pradesh**
