**Compliance** means **following rules, laws, standards, or requests**.

### In Different Contexts:

1. **General meaning:**
    - _Compliance_ is the act of **agreeing to or obeying** a rule, request, or command.
    - **Example:**  
        _He showed full compliance with the teacher’s instructions._

2. **Legal/Regulatory context:**
    - _Compliance_ means following **laws and regulations** set by governments or authorities.
    - **Example:**  
        _The company was fined for non-compliance with environmental laws._

3. **Corporate/Workplace context:**
    - Companies often have _compliance departments_ to ensure employees follow **internal policies and external legal requirements**.
    - **Example:**  
        _Compliance training is mandatory for all new employees._

4. **Medical context:**
    - Refers to how well a patient **follows a prescribed treatment or medication plan**.
    - **Example:**  
        _Low compliance with medication can lead to poor health outcomes._
