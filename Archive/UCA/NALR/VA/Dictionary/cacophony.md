**"Cacophony"** means a **harsh, jarring, and unpleasant mixture of sounds**.

---

### 📌 Example:

- _The cacophony of car horns and shouting made it hard to concentrate._
- _The classroom erupted in a cacophony of laughter and chatter._

---

**Synonyms:**  
noise, racket, discord, dissonance, uproar.

**Opposite:**  
**Euphony** – which means a pleasant or harmonious sound.
