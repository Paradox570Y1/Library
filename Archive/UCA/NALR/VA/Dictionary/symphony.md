### 🎼 1. **A long musical composition**, usually for a full orchestra, typically with multiple sections (called movements).

- _Beethoven's Fifth Symphony is one of the most famous symphonies in the world._

---

### 🎶 2. **A harmonious arrangement of sounds or elements** (used metaphorically).

- _The sunrise over the mountains was a symphony of colors._

---

**Synonyms (for music):** composition, orchestral piece  
**Synonyms (figurative):** harmony, blend, coordination

**Opposite (in sound quality):** **Cacophony** – harsh or jarring mixture of sounds.
