**"Dubious"** means:

### 1. **Doubtful or unsure**

Used when someone is uncertain about something.
- _She looked dubious about the plan._

### 2. **Questionable or suspicious**

Used when something seems dishonest or not to be trusted.
- _He made a lot of money through dubious means._

**Synonyms:** suspicious, uncertain, doubtful, questionable, shady.
