## 📘 1. **Peculiar**

### 🔹 **Definition:**

**Peculiar** means **strange**, **odd**, or **unusual**, often in a way that attracts attention or curiosity.

> It can also mean **specific to something**, depending on context.

---

### 🔹 **Examples in Sentences:**

1. _He has a **peculiar** way of speaking that makes everyone listen._
    
2. _There’s a **peculiar** smell in the room — like burnt toast._
    
3. _That bird is **peculiar** to this island and found nowhere else._  
    → _(Here, “peculiar” means “unique or specific to.”)_
    

---

### 🔹 **Synonyms:**

- Strange
    
- Unusual
    
- Odd
    
- Curious
    
- Unique (contextual)
    

---

### 🔹 **Memory Tip:**

> Think of **“peculiar”** as something that **makes you pause and notice**, because it’s **not normal**.
