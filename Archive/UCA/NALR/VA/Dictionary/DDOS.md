### ✅ **DDOS Meaning:**

**DDOS** stands for **Distributed Denial of Service**.

---

### 🔍 **What Is a DDOS Attack?**

A **DDOS attack** is a type of **cyberattack** where **multiple computers** (often hijacked ones) are used to **flood a server or network with excessive traffic**, so that it **crashes or becomes too slow** to function for real users.
