### 🔹 **Definition:**

**Scoot** is a **verb** that means to:

1. **Move quickly**, often in a **hurried or playful way**
    
2. **Slide or shift position**, especially in a small space
    

It’s usually **informal and light-hearted** in tone.

---

### 🔹 **Examples in Sentences:**

1. _He **scooted** over to make room on the bench._  
    → _(Shifted or slid sideways)_
    
2. _The child **scooted** across the floor on a toy car._  
    → _(Moved quickly or playfully)_
    
3. _When the bell rang, the students **scooted** out of the classroom._  
    → _(Hurried away)_
    

---

### 🔹 **Other Forms:**

- **Present:** scoot
    
- **Past:** scooted
    
- **Participle:** scooted
    
- **Noun (less common):** a **scoot** = a short, quick movement
    

---

### 🔹 **Synonyms:**

- Slide
    
- Glide
    
- Dash
    
- Zip
    
- Skedaddle (informal)
    

---

### 🔹 **Memory Tip:**

> Think of someone on a **scooter** — they **move fast and slide around** — that’s **scoot**!
