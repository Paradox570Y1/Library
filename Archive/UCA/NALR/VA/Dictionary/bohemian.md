
## 📕 2. **Bohemian**

### 🔹 **Definition:**

**Bohemian** refers to a person who lives **unconventionally**, often connected with the **arts**, **freedom**, and **non-materialistic values**.  
It can also describe a **style** that’s **artsy, relaxed, and eclectic**.

---

### 🔹 **Examples in Sentences:**

1. _She lived a **bohemian** lifestyle, traveling, painting, and rejecting modern norms._
    
2. _The café had a **bohemian** vibe — with mismatched furniture and indie music playing._
    
3. _Bohemian fashion includes flowing dresses, beads, earthy colors, and artistic patterns._
    

---

### 🔹 **Synonyms:**

- Artistic
- Nonconformist
- Free-spirited
- Unorthodox
- Hippie (informal, similar in spirit)

---

### 🔹 **Memory Tip:**

> **Bohemian** = **"Be free + Human"** → A person living **freely and creatively**, outside society’s rules.
