### ✂️ **Pruning** — Meaning, Explanation, and Examples

---

### 🔹 **Definition:**

**Pruning** is a **verb** that primarily means:

1. **To trim or cut away** dead or unnecessary parts of a plant, tree, or bush to help it grow better.
2. **Figuratively**, it means **removing unnecessary elements** to improve efficiency or clarity.

---

### 🔹 **Examples in Sentences:**

#### 🌿 **Literal (Gardening):**

- _She spent the afternoon **pruning** the rose bushes in the garden._
- _It’s important to **prune** fruit trees every season for healthy growth._

#### 🧠 **Figurative (Ideas / Systems):**

- _The editor is **pruning** the article to make it more concise._
- _The company is **pruning** unprofitable departments to cut costs._

---

### 🔹 **Synonyms:**

|Literal (Gardening)|Figurative (Improvement)|
|---|---|
|Trim|Eliminate|
|Cut back|Simplify|
|Remove|Refine|
|Lop off|Streamline|

---

### 🔹 **Memory Tip:**

> **Pruning = Improving by Removing**  
> Think of cutting off the **dead parts** so the **whole thing grows better** — whether it's a plant, an idea, or a business plan.
