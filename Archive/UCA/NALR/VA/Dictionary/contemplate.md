**"Contemplate"** means to **think deeply and carefully** about something, often for a long time, or to **consider doing something seriously**.

---

### 🔹 Meanings:

1. **To think deeply about something:**
    - _She contemplated the meaning of life._
        
2. **To consider doing something in the future:**
    - _He’s contemplating a career change._
        

---

**Synonyms:** ponder, reflect, consider, deliberate, meditate.
