

- Presentation
	- Problem – What issue are you solving?
	- Solution – How are you solving it?
	- Intended impact – What positive change will your solution bring?
	- Resources – What tools, technologies, materials, or manpower are needed?
	- Scalability – How can your solution grow or be implemented on a larger scale?

-  One-Page Poster / Infographic (A4 PDF)
	- The topic/title of your project
	- Visuals (icons, charts, photos, or sketches)
		- Coffee life cycle

- Story Board
	1. Title & Objective – project name and main goal
	2. Overview – short project summers.
	3. Key Steps / Scenes – main phases or milestones
	4. Visuals – sketches, mockups, or images for each step
	5. Description – brief notes on what happens in each stage
	6. Timeline – order and estimated duration
	7. Stakeholders – people or teams involved
	8. Deliverables – outputs or results from each stage.
