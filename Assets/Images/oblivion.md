The word **"oblivion"** refers to the state of **being forgotten**, **unaware**, or **completely unknown**, often in the context of memory loss or fading into nothingness.

---

### 🔹 **Definitions of Oblivion:**

1. **Literal Meaning:**
    
    > The state of **being unaware** or **unconscious** of what is happening.
    
2. **Figurative Meaning:**
    
    > The state of **being forgotten**, **ignored**, or **no longer recognized** by society or memory.
    

---

### 🔹 **Root & Origin:**

- Comes from Latin **“oblivio”** meaning “forgetfulness.”
- Related to the verb **“to obliterate”** (to wipe out completely).

---

### 🔹 **Examples of 'Oblivion' in Sentences:**

1. **Unconsciousness or forgetfulness:**
    
    > After the accident, she lay in a coma, lost in **oblivion** for weeks.
    
2. **Being forgotten over time:**
    
    > Many ancient civilizations have faded into **oblivion**, their stories buried under layers of earth.
    
3. **Deliberate escape:**
    
    > He drank himself into **oblivion**, trying to forget the pain of his past.
    
4. **Artistic or career downfall:**
    
    > Once a famous actor, he now lives in **oblivion**, with no one recognizing his name.
    
5. **Historical:**
    
    > The poetry of that era risked falling into **oblivion** until scholars revived interest in it.
    

---

### 🔹 **Common Phrases:**

| Phrase                          | Meaning                                                  |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Fade into oblivion**          | To be forgotten or no longer remembered.                 |
| **Sink into oblivion**          | To become completely forgotten or lost.                  |
| **Drink oneself into oblivion** | To drink alcohol until unconsciousness or forgetfulness. |
| **Rescue from oblivion**        | To save something from being forgotten.                  |

---

### 🔹 **Synonyms of Oblivion:**

- Forgetfulness
- Unconsciousness
- Neglect
- Obscurity
- Amnesia

---

### 🔹 **Opposites (Antonyms):**

- Awareness
- Remembrance
- Recognition
- Fame
- Notoriety

---

### 🔹 **Memory Tip:**

> Think of **oblivion** like a black hole—**once something falls in, it’s forgotten or erased from memory**.
