
- `Supervised Learning :` Every training sample from the dataset has a corresponding label or output value associated with it. As a result, the algorithm learns to predict labels or output values.
  ![[Pasted image 20240626093806.png|200]]
  It can be used to predict the Selling price of house or classify object in an image.
  
- `Unsupervised Learning :` There are no labels for the training data. A machine learning algorithm tries to learn the underlying patterns or distributions that govern the data.
  ![[Pasted image 20240626094019.png| 200]]
  >__Remember, In supervised and unsupervised learning, models inspect data to discover patterns then human uses patterns learned by the model to gain kneel understanding or make predictions.__
  
- `Reinforcement Learning :` Learning what actions to take in a given situation to maximize reward on the way to reaching a specific goal. 
  Ex:- It is similar to how you train your pet. If your dog do something you wanted to do you might reward it with a treat. But if it does something you don't want it to do then you might correct it with a small penalty like raising your voice. Dog learns to do the things that give a treat and avoid doing things that get a correction.
