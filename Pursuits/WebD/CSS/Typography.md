# Famous Font Families
## Serif Typeface
![[Pasted image 20240803091117.png|200]]
- Choosing a serif typeface you are making your design look more serious, more authoritative and little bit older as well.
- It is good if you are designing letterhead for a legal company or a magazine for architectural design.

### Subdivisions of sans-serif typeface
![[Pasted image 20240803091909.png|300]]
- It is more approachable, novel and contemporary.
- Humanist typeface is also know as highly readable type face.
- More used for body text.
- Fonts belonging in Humanist typeface category(highly readable fonts) :
	- Gill Sans
	- Tahoma
	- Verdana
