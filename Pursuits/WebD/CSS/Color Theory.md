# Picking Colors
![[Pasted image 20240803085808.png|400]]

- Choosing adjacent colors can be good for nav bar, body of website, logo and its background.
- It is not good for standing out.

![[Pasted image 20240803085943.png|400]]
- To make your design pop, stand out choose opposite color.
- Do not try style text and its background with it. Just keep it to logos, icons.
