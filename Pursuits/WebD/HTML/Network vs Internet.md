### What is Network?
- A network is a collection of computers, servers, mainframes, network devices, or other devices that are connected to each other to allow sharing of data and resources.

### Types of Network
- **Local Area Network (LAN)**: A network that is confined to a small geographic area, like a single building or a campus. It usually involves a few computers and devices.
- **Wide Area Network (WAN)**: A network that covers a broad area, such as a city, country, or even the globe. The Internet is the largest WAN.
- **Metropolitan Area Network (MAN)**: A network that spans a city or a large campus.
- **Personal Area Network (PAN)**: A network for interconnecting devices centered around an individual person's workspace, such as a Bluetooth network.
